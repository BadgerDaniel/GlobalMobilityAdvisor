# Policy Agent - Input Collection Questions

## Required Information for Policy Analysis

1. **Employee Category**
   - Question: "What is the employee's category?"
   - Options: Regular employee, Contractor, Executive, Intern

2. **Assignment Type**
   - Question: "What type of assignment is being considered?"
   - Options: Permanent transfer, Long-term assignment (1-3 years), Short-term assignment (3-12 months), Project-based assignment

3. **Origin Country/Location**
   - Question: "What is the employee's current country and location?"
   - Format: City, Country (e.g., "Chicago, USA")

4. **Destination Country/Location**
   - Question: "What is the destination country and location?"
   - Format: City, Country (e.g., "Berlin, Germany")

5. **Assignment Duration**
   - Question: "What is the expected duration of the assignment?"
   - Format: Specific timeframe (e.g., "18 months", "2 years", "permanent")

6. **Visa/Work Permit Status**
   - Question: "What is the current visa/work permit status?"
   - Options: Needs new visa, Has existing visa, Citizen/permanent resident, Transfer within company visa

7. **Family Accompaniment**
   - Question: "Will family members be accompanying the employee?"
   - Options: No family, Spouse only, Spouse and children, Children only

8. **Previous International Assignments**
   - Question: "Has the employee had previous international assignments?"
   - Options: No previous assignments, 1 previous assignment, Multiple previous assignments

9. **Business Justification**
   - Question: "What is the business justification for this assignment?"
   - Examples: Critical project needs, Skills transfer, Market expansion, Leadership development

10. **Budget/Cost Constraints**
    - Question: "Are there any specific budget or cost constraints?"
    - Format: Budget range or specific limitations

## Policy Analysis Process
Once all information is collected, our AI engine will:
- Check eligibility requirements
- Identify compliance requirements
- Review visa and immigration needs
- Assess policy approval requirements
- Provide step-by-step guidance for assignment setup
