# Compensation Agent - Input Collection Questions

## Required Information for Compensation Calculation

1. **Origin Location** 
   - Question: "What is the employee's current location (city, country)?"
   - Format: City, Country (e.g., "New York, USA")

2. **Destination Location**
   - Question: "Where will the employee be relocating to (city, country)?"
   - Format: City, Country (e.g., "London, UK")

3. **Current Base Salary**
   - Question: "What is the employee's current annual base salary and currency?"
   - Format: Amount + Currency (e.g., "$75,000 USD")

4. **Employee Level/Grade**
   - Question: "What is the employee's level or grade?"
   - Options: Junior, Mid-level, Senior, Manager, Director, Executive

5. **Family Status**
   - Question: "What is the employee's family situation?"
   - Options: Single, Married (no children), Married with children, Single parent

6. **Assignment Type**
   - Question: "What type of assignment is this?"
   - Options: Permanent relocation, Long-term assignment (1-3 years), Short-term assignment (3-12 months)

7. **Assignment Duration** (if temporary)
   - Question: "What is the expected duration of the assignment?"
   - Format: Number of months/years

8. **Housing Preference**
   - Question: "What housing arrangement is preferred?"
   - Options: Company-provided housing, Housing allowance, Employee finds own housing

9. **Special Circumstances**
   - Question: "Are there any special circumstances to consider?"
   - Examples: Hardship location, high cost of living, remote location, etc.

## Calculation Process
Once all information is collected, our AI engine will:
- Calculate cost-of-living adjustments
- Determine appropriate allowances (housing, hardship, etc.)
- Factor in tax implications
- Provide total compensation package estimate
